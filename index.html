About Me

With a background in science education both abroad and here in the Bay Area, I am a technical support specialist working on online gaming, social good, and anything else that I can help passionate, visionary designers bring to life.


Coding is very exciting and rewarding to me because it allows me to deconstruct my intuitive thinking into discrete, testable parts. Building up an interlocking code base is incredibly satisfying, and looking back on a finished app with many moving parts that I fully understand is one of the most rewarding work experiences I've had.


I'm also fond of working in groups and enjoy talking about new approaches to challenging problems in pursuit of a designer's layout or idea. Everyone brings different skills to full stack development, and being a trusted part of the team (especially supporting on back end logic) drives me to excel every day.
